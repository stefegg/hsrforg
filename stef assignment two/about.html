About Me

I used to be a property manager by trade. It had it's challenges, but was not very fulfilling. Problems were rarely solved permanently, and the clients were terrible.

I decided late in 2016 that I wanted a change. But I didn't think there was much hope, time and money were both in short supply.So I saved, and saved, and saved. And when 2017 was finished, so was I as a property manager. I quit!

But I still didn't know what I wanted to do yet - however a chance meeting with an old friend reminded me how much I used to love making websites as a kid. I'd always wanted to take a class or a boot camp... but again, time and money were always missing... until now!

So I'm taking to test the waters - I'm loving it so far! It's really invigorating to be studying something i'm passionate about, and I'm loving devouring all the material.

I also really like to travel! Most recently I visited Monterey, California. The photo on the main page was taken when I visited New Orleans for Voodoo Fest in 2016.
I live in Brooklyn with my girlfriend and my cat.
